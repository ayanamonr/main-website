What's Product Management?

Make things happen.

Empathize

Identify problem spaces and market opportunities. Hold empathy with the people to uderstand their needs.

Envision

Define the reason why to build a product, and what to build. Prioritize and solve the most important problems first.

Engage

Bring in cross-functional teams to turn an idea into a launched product. Lead the teams in realizing long-term vision of the product.
